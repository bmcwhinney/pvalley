Who We Are

Cultivating Nature in Borne

Welcome to Paradise Valley Garden & Nursery, a sanctuary rooted in the fertile volcanic soil of Borne, St. John. More than just a nursery, we are a destination where nature, history, and community converge.

Home to vibrant resident peacocks and historic estate artifacts, our grounds offer a glimpse into Dominica's rich heritage. We are committed to sustainability, propagating rare Anthurium dominicense and exotic tropicals that thrive in our unique island climate.
